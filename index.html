Delaunay Triangulation Splitter

By Alan Pierce

Add red and blue points to the area below, and then hit enter and press space over and over to step through and algorithm that finds the Delaunay triangulation of just the red points and the Delaunay triangulation of just the blue points.

Note that this is still a work in progress. There are plenty of known potential improvements and possible bugs.


You need to have Java enabled to use this.

The complete algorithm, and its analysis, are described in the paper "Splitting a Delaunay Triangulation in Linear Time", by B. Chazelle, O. Devillers, F. Hurtado, M. Mora, V. Sacristan, and M. Teillaud.
